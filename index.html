About

A lifetime spent
decoding the mind

Dr. Parag Jasani is an independent researcher and the creator of the Dichotomised Operating System (DOS) Model — a psychological and evolutionary framework that reveals the mechanistic functions behind human thought, behaviour, and social conflict.

Unlike conventional neuroscience, which maps the brain in terms of frequencies, signals, and regions, Dr. Jasani's approach treats the mind as a system — one whose hidden operating logic can be traced back to the process of natural selection. The result is not abstract philosophy but a precise, information-processing manual for the human mind.

His research spans over fifteen years and addresses questions that science has long left unanswered: why we dream, why we meditate, why we feel superior to others, why language exists, and what truly drives thought. Each answer is derived not from opinion but from first principles — from the deep logic of evolution itself.

His book Mechanisms of the Mind lays out the complete DOS Model. The Heal The World initiative, the purpose of this website, extends that framework outward — toward global peace, rooted in the conviction that understanding the mind is the only lasting path to resolving human conflict.

The Framework

The DOS Model
explained

The Dichotomised Operating System Model treats the human mind as a biological operating system — one with two distinct processing layers whose interaction generates every thought, emotion, and behaviour we experience.

01

Two Hemispheres, Two Systems

The left hemisphere uses direct logic (System 1) while the right hemisphere uses contextual logic (System 2). Together they form a dichotomised operating architecture — the brain processes every interaction through both simultaneously, optimising for survival and reproduction.

02

Evolution as the Root Cause

All complex human behaviours — social conflicts, emotional states, biases, relationships — are driven by a singular underlying evolutionary goal. Natural selection is not just biology; it is the hidden operating system running your daily mental life.

03

The Self as a Software Agent

The DOS Model treats the “self” as a specific mental mechanism — a software agent designed to process information in ways that optimise natural selection. Understanding this agent is the key to understanding why we behave the way we do.

04

Logic Over Sentiment

By understanding the precise information-processing logic of the mind, we can replace reactive, emotion-driven conflict with clear, causal understanding. Every bias, every prejudice, every social rift has a mechanistic explanation — and therefore, a resolution.

05

A Causal, Not Correlational, Approach

Conventional brain science accumulates correlational data — frequencies, activations, genetic markers. The DOS Model asks why. It is a causal, systems-thinking framework that reveals the root causes of mental phenomena rather than merely cataloguing them.

06

Universal Application

From meditation and sleep to language, gender differences, and the superiority bias — the DOS Model provides coherent, first-principles explanations for the full spectrum of human mental experience. One framework. Every phenomenon.

The Initiative

Heal The World

If we understand the precise operating logic of the human mind, we can replace conflict with comprehension — not as a sentiment, but as a fact.

The Heal The World project is Dr. Jasani's global initiative to achieve peace through understanding. It is built on the belief that social conflicts — between individuals, communities, and nations — are not inevitable. They are the predictable output of a misunderstood mind.

By making the DOS Model accessible to the world, this project aims to give every person the tools to understand their own biases, decode the motivations of others, and resolve conflict at its root — rather than managing its symptoms.
